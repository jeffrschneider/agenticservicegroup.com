Departmental Relationships

The success of the Agentic Service Group depends on strong, well-defined partnerships across the enterprise. These relationships establish clear accountability boundaries, enable coordinated operations, and ensure unified governance of autonomous systems.

Each department plays a critical role in the autonomous enterprise. Click on any department below to explore the operational matrix that defines how ASG influences their work and what responsibilities they retain.

The introduction of AI agents fundamentally reshapes the workforce landscape. HR must evolve to manage the human-agent collaboration, address role transitions, and ensure employees are empowered rather than displaced by autonomous systems.

Key Role: Domain Change Specialist

The Domain Change Specialist serves as the critical bridge between AI capabilities and human workforce adaptation. This role focuses on identifying how AI agents will transform specific business domains and orchestrating the human side of that transition.

  • • Maps existing job functions to AI-augmented workflows
  • • Designs reskilling and upskilling pathways for affected employees
  • • Facilitates change management and communication strategies
  • • Monitors workforce sentiment and adoption metrics
  • • Ensures ethical considerations in workforce transformation
Operational Matrix

  • • Capability impact assessments
  • • Human-agent collaboration models
HR Responsible

  • • Training program development
  • • Role redefinition & career pathing
